Yes, we provide separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with comfortable, safe living spaces, nutritious home-like meals, 24/7 security and supervision, and a study-friendly environment with all basic amenities included. We also offer reliable and safe transportation for day-scholar students covering major areas across Vijayawada.

Our highly qualified and experienced faculty specialize in both intermediate board syllabus and competitive exam preparation.
